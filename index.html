About HyperKcal

HyperKcal was created by a passionate gym enthusiast who once struggled to keep track of macros and meals. This app seamlessly combines calorie counting, AI-driven meal planning, and progress tracking to make your fitness journey smoother and more efficient. Whether you're starting out or you're a seasoned gym-goer, HyperKcal is here to help you fuel smart, train hard, and stay on track!

"Fuel Smart. Train Hard. Track Everything."
